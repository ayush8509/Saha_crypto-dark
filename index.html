About Saha

Saha is a multi-utility DeFi token with a community focus that is part of the growing Saha ecosystem. The SAHA Team's objective is to make Blockchain technology effective, simple, and accessible to the whole world's population. The blockchain, in our opinion, is a response to the consolidation of authority, restoring people autonomy and independence. SAHA was created with the goal of providing maximum utility to not just its community members, but the whole globe. With the cooperation of other nation’s governments, a large amount of the money earned will be used to benefit the society through our "WE CARE" Initiative. Our goal is to create at least 1 million employment opportunities in the next three years while enhancing the use of our cryptocurrency and assisting the unemployed. These job openings will be in our Saha headquarters, the Employ Beggars campaign, the Fight Against Pollution Campaign, and, of course, our Sahaverse, which has a goal of creating 10 million jobs in the next five years. All community members and whitelisted accounts will not only make great returns on their investments, but they will also be able to participate in our many campaigns and track their progress.
